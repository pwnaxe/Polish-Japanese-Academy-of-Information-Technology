PJAIT Career Fair spring edition - April 20, 2023! Students and Graduates, do not miss the opportunity to talk with leading employers
and other attractions during the Fair.

We will see you from 9:30 to 16:30 in building A.
About the event: The PJAIT Career Fair is a cyclical event organized on the campus of the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology. During the Career Fair, students and graduates have the opportunity to talk directly with companies offering various types of internships and positions. The fair is also a great opportunity to establish contacts with representatives of companies from various industries.
What awaits you at the spring edition of the Fair? - job offers and internships
- many representatives of employers with whom you will have the opportunity to talk
- quick consultations with career advisors of the Academic Career Office of PJAIT
- workshops and lectures accompanying the Caeer Fair (21.04), as well as certificates confirming your participation in the workshops
- gamification for students: prizes to be won!
